Rean Schwarzer Collectible

From the popular game, The Legend of Heroes series by Nihon Falcom, the protagonist also known as the “Ashen Chevalier,” Rean Schwarzer is coming out as a figure!

With his instructor outfit from The Legend of Heroes Trails of Cold Steel III fluttering behind him, Rean comes to life as he strikes a powerful attack with his tachi in the Eight Leaves One Blade style.

Details

The delicate details in his outfit and long sword are accurately recreated, and the base is printed with his long-standing companion, Valimar, the Ashen Knight who fought alongside Rean for many years.
